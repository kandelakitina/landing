Boticelli's Web Site

Botticelli's landing page is a digital tribute to Sandro Botticelli, showcasing masterpieces like "The Birth of Venus" and "Primavera." It features a minimalist design with a soft color palette, echoing Botticelli's delicate hues. High-resolution images of his artwork allow visitors to explore intricate details.
